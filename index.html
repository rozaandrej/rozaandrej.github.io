What are the financial benefits of solar energy?

Reduced electricity bills: By installing solar panels, homeowners and businesses can generate their own electricity, leading to a significant reduction in monthly electricity bills.

What are the environmental benefits of solar energy?

Renewable and clean energy: Solar energy is a renewable energy source that harnesses the power of the sun. It produces electricity without emitting greenhouse gases, air pollutants, or other harmful byproducts, making it a clean energy alternative to fossil fuels.

What is net metering?

Net metering is a billing system used by utility companies that allows customers with renewable energy systems, such as solar panels, to send excess energy back to the grid and receive credits on their electricity bill.
